I'm currently a fourth-year undergraduate transfer student at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering & Computer Science. Before transferring, I dabbled in graphic design, 2D and 3D visual effects, and some computer programming. I find that my personality deeply resonates with the jack-of-all-trades master of one mentality. In other words, breadth is my bread and butter but systems engineering is what gives me direction. I have a passion for space exploration and autonomous systems. By the age of 20, I had worked on an earth-orbiting satellite mission and Mars 2020 Rover during my internship at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory. I also spent over 200 hours designing, building, and testing an autonomous quadrotor from scratch. In the future, I'd like to become one of many engineers who boldy endeavor to push humankind's technological cabalities to unexplored frontiers.
